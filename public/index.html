#dalstondigitaldrinks

Dalston Digital is a very loose group of folk that meet up from time to time to have a beer and talk design and technology, usually in the Dalston/Shoreditch/Islington triangle.

We are designers, programmers and business people who build the web and want to keep making it better.

This web page is an effort to increase our exposure and perhaps even foster some between meet up collaborations.

#participants

  • Well Informed Limited—a product development team running a bootstrapped software as a service business.
  • With Associates—a digital agency. In a nutshell, we build, design and plan websites and apps.

Fork the repository and add your details here. Let's try to keep them in alphabetical order.

    #about

    From what I can gather Dalston Digital Drinks was started by Alex+Alex as a way to meet more people doing the same sorts of things as they do and to perhaps meet some freelancers to hire. There was a loose intention that perhaps there might be an informal presentation at the start of the meet up but that didn't ever quite work out.

    Meet ups now take place from time to time organised by whoever takes charge and creates a WhenShouldWe poll.

    Site currently maintained by @timharding.
